About this page
Judy's recipe site honors the work that went into collecting, preserving, and sharing family favorites.
Where did this come from?

This page was created and is maintained by Matthew Maupin, grandson of Judith Maupin. All recipes are from Judy's recipe book as maintained over the years by Judy and Gib, with the help of many family members including Mike Trulock (original archiving), Joe and Nora Maupin (2025 re-organization), and the countless friends and family that shared their versions of recipes or contributed to the original collection accumulated by Judy over the years.

The recipes are maintained in a Google Sheets database. To request additions, changes, or share suggestions, please email matthewmaupin@gmail.com.
